About Me

Hi, I’m Madison — a licensed stylist passionate about helping clients feel confident, refreshed, and beautiful every time they leave my chair. With a focus on personalized color, modern cuts, and healthy hair care, I strive to create a relaxing experience where every client feels heard and cared for. Whether you’re looking for a subtle refresh or a complete transformation, my goal is to bring your vision to life while helping you maintain healthy, beautiful hair. When I’m not behind the chair, I’m staying up to date on the latest trends, techniques, and products to continue giving my clients the best experience possible.
